We are Rossio Square.

We are a small but strong team based in Silicon Valley, California. We are working hard to help people find the best deals for clothing, accessories and home furnishings. Click around to learn about our story and how we got started.

Design

Whether you are shopping at home in front of your laptop or you are at the mall with your smartphone, our goal is to ensure your experience on Rossio Square is engaging, beautiful, intuitive and exciting!

Value

Finding the best deals doesn't have to amount to a treasure hunt or finding a needle in the haystack! Our goal is to help you find the best deals quickly and easily.We want to make this your everyday experience

Technology

We believe that we are on the cusp of a technology transition that will leverage things like location awareness, user preferences and real time inventory status to make shopping a very different experience.

The Problem

We found ourselves paying retail price for our favorite brand of jeans at the local mall when it was actually on sale online.

Due to our busy schedules, we were constantly scrambling to buy gifts for friends and family at list price when attractive deals were available online at the same time.


We are not the only people with these problems. A number of solutions already exist today. But we find them to be cluttered, impersonal and deliver a very poor user experience.

The Solution

The founders have a strong background in content discovery, search and classification and were able to develop the technology necessary to solve this problem for them, their friends and family.

It just seemed like a good idea to help other people enjoy the same benefits

Rossio Square was launched with two simple objectives (1) Help users save money and time by helping them find the best deals for apparel, accessories and home furnishings and (2) Make shopping for deals an enjoyable experience.
